The Renaissance Child Is Ready to Help

We started our platform in 2016 as a way to share our passion for helping children master the holistic, interpersonal, and unique skills that will help them learn and grow. Our curriculum is based on progressive, research-backed teaching methods like those implemented in the STEAM and Montessori learning approaches. Our team consists of highly-qualified and patient IT specialists, instructors, and teachers who are passionate about helping children just like yours thrive.

OUR CAMPS

There's no limit to learning at The Renaissance Child. We offer a range of NJ State Certified Summer Programs where your child can explore their intellectual curiosity in the safest, most capable hands. Each of our instructors is CPR certified and ready to implement a range of STEAM- based learning experiences and curricula.

Our camps include the Explorer's Camp for ages 3 to 5, the Technology Bootcamp for ages 8 to 14, the Robotics and STEAM Camp for ages 6 to 10, and the Days Off School Camps for kids aged 6 to 10.
